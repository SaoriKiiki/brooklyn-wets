ABOUT BK WETS

We Are the BK Wets

We were founded in 2015 after an overwhelming response to our New York Water Polo Meetup. We provide a place to scrimmage and a group to have a beer with after the scrimmage. Ages of members range from 18 to "None of your business." We're co-ed, and field teams for women's and co-ed games.

Our Philosophy

The game is the greatest teacher of the game. Our coaches like talking but refer watching you have fun. If you want advice on your game, ask the coaches. Drills are simple and short. We want you to play and have fun.

Be Part of Our Team

We are straight, gay, white, brown, black, Romanian, Canadian, Australian, etc. It's New York, so it's a diverse crew. It's a good time, and you should join us.

Practice Times

We practice Saturdays 2-3:30PM at Metropolitan Rec Center in Williamsburg. We also have a more instructional beinnger friendly practice at Mondays at 6pm at the Gertrude Ederle Recreation Center.

Urban Dolphins!

Our women's team is a combination of experienced and new players, nicknamed the Urban Dolphins. Our ladies like to play against the boys and against other women, scrimmaging with NYAC and Columbia. They also like to hand out outside of the pool (NO BOYS ALLOWED!).
